About YSR

"In the year 2000, a visionary engineer named Syed Fahad Rizvi and his a few team members embarked on a journey to make a positive impact on society.
Later on, this journey would evolve into a remarkable foundation, known as 'Young Social Reformers' or YSR."

Establishing the Foundation


Over the years, the foundation has grown and expanded its reach beyond the borders of Pakistan, that is YSR extends its helping hand to countries not only in Pakistan but also in Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, China, Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, Afghanistan, further in Nepal, Egypt, Turkey, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kenya, South Africa, and Italy."

YSR's Global Impact


YSR has just not been working tirelessly in 69 districts in Pakistan but also with local organizations in other countries. The foundation's working impact makes a specific difference in the lives of individuals and communities.

Sub-organizations of YSR


let's discuss this with the 4 YSR sub-organizations which include YSR Pakistan, YSR Educational and Professional Foundation, YSR Sports Foundation, and YSR Welfare Foundation which are working to create a comprehensive impact.

YSR in Action

Who We Are

Vision

Empowering young people with Education for their sustainable future and national progress.
They could empower themself with social norms and be able to fight against poverty, hunger, and get justice.

Mission

Bringing changes in society through education, and finance especially targeting rural areas where facilities are limited for academic knowledge, and fewer job facilities available.
We aim t0 instill a sense of curiosity, critical thinking, and a passion for lifelong learning.

AIM & Objective

YSR Pakistan strives for holistic community development, focusing on education accessibility, welfare services, sports promotion, and overall societal improvement.
